Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about Trees2You Christmas trees.

What sizes of trees do you have available?

We offer Nordmann Fir trees from 5ft to 9ft tall, with 6ft being our most popular family size.

How far do you deliver?

We deliver within 15km of our farm in Glynn, Wexford. Contact us to check if we deliver to your area.

When should I order my tree?

We recommend ordering by early December to ensure availability of your preferred size, especially for deliveries.

Do you offer tree stands?

Yes! We have premium metal tree stands available for purchase that provide excellent stability and water capacity.
