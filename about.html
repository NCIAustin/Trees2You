Growing Dreams Since 2019

Trees2You began as a passion project born from our love of Christmas and the beautiful Wexford countryside. What started as a small family farm has evolved into something special - a place where families come to find not just a tree, but the beginning of their Christmas magic.

We chose Nordmann Fir trees because we believe every family deserves a beautiful, safe Christmas tree that brings joy without the worry of sharp needles or constant cleanup. As parents ourselves, we understand what matters most.

Over five years, we've had the privilege of serving over 1,000 families across Wexford, creating lasting memories and friendships along the way.
