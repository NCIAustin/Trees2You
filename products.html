Why Nordmann Fir Trees

Nordmann Fir is the premium choice for families who want beauty, safety, and convenience. These exceptional trees offer everything you need for the perfect Christmas.

  • Soft, non-drop needles safe for children and pets
  • Excellent needle retention throughout the season
  • Beautiful, symmetrical shape with strong branches
  • Fresh pine scent without being overpowering
  • Sustainable growing practices
