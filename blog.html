Essential Tree Care Tips

Key tips every Trees2You customer should know to keep their Nordmann Fir tree looking beautiful and lasting throughout the Christmas season.

Check water daily - trees can drink up to a gallon per day when fresh

Fresh cut the trunk before setup for maximum water absorption

Keep away from heat sources like radiators and fireplaces

Use LED lights to reduce heat and extend tree freshness
